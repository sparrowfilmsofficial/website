Privacy
Policy.

At Sparrow Films, we treat your data with the same precision and respect we bring to our cinematic productions. Your privacy is not a secondary concern; it is a fundamental right.

This Privacy Policy describes how Sparrow Films ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you visit our website or engage with our production services.

1. Our Core Privacy Promise

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third-party companies for marketing purposes. Your brand strategy, project details, and contact information stay within Sparrow Films. We believe that professional collaboration requires absolute trust.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and brand name when you fill out our "Start Project" form.
  • Project Briefs: Any details you share regarding your marketing goals, vision, and production requirements.
  • Communication Data: Records of your interactions with us via email, WhatsApp, or contact forms.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for the purpose of delivering high-end visual architecture and viral content:

  • To contact you regarding your project inquiries.
  • To customize our production strategies to your brand's specific needs.
  • To send project updates and deliver final assets.
  • To improve our website experience and agency services.

4. Data Protection & Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to secure your personal data. Access to client information is restricted to the five core team members of the Sparrow Films collective on a need-to-know basis.

5. Third-Party Services

While we do not share your data for marketing, we use secure third-party tools to facilitate our operations (e.g., hosting providers, project management software). These providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and are prohibited from using your data for any other purpose.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal information at any time. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us directly at contact@sparrowfilms.in.

7. Updates to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The "Last Updated" date at the top will always reflect the most recent version.
